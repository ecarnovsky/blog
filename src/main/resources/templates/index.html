Hello, and Welcome to My Blog

This blog was made to be a place where I could write about my experiences building software projects. Who am I? My name’s Elizabeth Carnovsky, and I’m a student with a passion for making beautiful and responsive websites. This website was coded and designed by me, and hopefully will have added functionality in the near future. I plan to work on improving the mobile view next. Please tell me what you think about the design! It was really hard to choose the colors, but eventually I settled on a nature theme. If you want to contact me, there’s a link to both my GitHub and my LinkedIn at the bottom of the page. I look forward to hearing your thoughts about the design.

As for my next project, I’m not sure what I want it to be. I would love to do a plant database website where users can contribute information and pictures of plants. I know several such websites already exists, but each one has its own issues. I want my website to have a focus on community. Forums divided by location could help gardeners find out about good local plant shops and events. I have a lot to think about in regards to how the website should be designed, and what features users would want most.

This is the end of my first blog post. Thank you for reading!
